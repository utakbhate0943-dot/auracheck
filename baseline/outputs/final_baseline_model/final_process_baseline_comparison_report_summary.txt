Model performance metrics
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Definitions:
• Accuracy: proportion of all predictions that are correct.
• Kappa: chance-corrected agreement between predictions and true labels.
• Macro_Recall: mean of per-class recall values.
• Log_Loss: probability error metric; lower is better and penalizes overconfident wrong predictions.
• F1_Macro: mean of class-wise F1 (harmonic mean of precision and recall).
• Recall_VeryLow/Low/Moderate/High: class-specific sensitivity TP/(TP+FN).
